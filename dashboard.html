Algorithm

  1. Patient enter the clinic
  2. Ask patient for what are his need?
  3. Get information of the patient
  4. Set a meeting for the doctor
  5. Ask the patient to wait for his turn
  6. Doctor meets the patient
  7. Doctor interview the patient
  8. Doctor diagnose the patient
  9. If the doctor gives prescription proceed to step 10, else go to Step 11
  10. Buy medicine / Go to laboratory
  11. Go to nurse station
  12. Pay the clinical fee and go home

Pseudocode

BEGIN

Input patient information

Assign patient for a corresponding doctor

Input patient medical record

IF

Doctor gives diagnostic/laboraties

Go to laboratory

THEN

Proceed to computation of clinical fee

ELSE

Compute the patient's clinical fee

Give patient corresponding clinical fee

END
